My Interests & Skills

Quantum Computing & Information

Today's world needs faster computing power to solve problems which are extremely hard to perform even on a super computer such as simulating a many-body quantum system that requires exponential amount of resouces. From drug designing to solving large scale Combinatorial Optimization problems, Quantum computer has the potential to outperform classical computers to some extent. My research interests broadly lies, but not limited to, in topics such as

  • Foundation of Quantum mechanics
  • Quantum Metrology & Information
  • NISQ Algorithms
  • Quantum Machine Learning
  • Combinatorial Optimization

Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning have also played a key role in solving computationally challenging problems. Machine Learning tools have been widely used in problems related to Physics like quantum many-body physics, fluid mechanics. I am also intrigued by its application in medical science such as cancer detection, drug designing etc.
